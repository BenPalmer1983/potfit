Interactions

potfit supports various short range effective potentials. These potentials are defined either by tabulated functions or analytic parameters and are read from a potential file. The potentials supported are central pair potentials, and Embedded Atom Method (EAM) potentials for metals.

Since the computation of the forces is the most time-critical part of a molecular dynamics simulation, support for each type of interaction has to be enabled explicitly by a compilation option.
